"Blade Runner"

Blade Runner is a film loosely based on Philip K. Dick's book, "Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep?". The film, it's sequels, and spin offs, are a neo noir set in a dystopian future. It raises several philosophical questions such as: What defines being a human? What is the true nature of consciousness? What happens when the created exceed their creators?
