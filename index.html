"Blade Runner"

"Blade Runner is a film loosely based on Philip K. Dick's book "Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep?". The film and it's sequels and spin offs, are neo noir set in a dystopian future. It raises several philosophical questions such as: What defines being a human? What is the true nature of consciousness? What happens when the created exeed their creators?"
